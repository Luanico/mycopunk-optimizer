    Need Custom Shapes?

    Since i didn't unlock every upgrade yet, and they also sometimes change shape in updates, you might not find the upgrade you want. That's why i added a shape maker that you can use

    How it works:

    The program works by having a folder with all the shapes, sorted in their different weapons/characters. You can download the base folder, then go the the shapemaker, create the upgrades you need, and save them in the right folder. Once you have all your shapes added, you can load your custom folder instead of the default one on this page.
